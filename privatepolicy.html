SMS/Text Messaging Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 20, 2026

Business Name: Precision BioVerify Solutions Service

At Precision BioVerify Solutions Service, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains how we handle SMS (text message) communications with our clients.

1. Consent to Receive SMS Messages

During a phone conversation, we will ask for your permission to send SMS messages to your mobile number. By agreeing, you consent to receive text messages related to our services.

  • You can opt out of future messages at any time
  • Message and data rates may apply
  • The number of messages may vary based on our conversations and services provided

2. Initial Opt-In Message

After you provide consent, you will receive an initial SMS message that includes all required disclosures.

  • Business identification
  • Opt-out instructions
  • Notification of message frequency and potential charges

3. How We Use SMS Communication

  • Confirm and remind you of appointments
  • Provide service updates and important notifications
  • Respond to your questions and requests
  • Communicate relevant information about your services

4. Opt-Out Instructions

You may opt out of receiving SMS messages at any time by replying STOP to any message.

5. Data Privacy & Protection

  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties
  • We do not convey your private data to outside organizations
  • Your information is used strictly for communication and service purposes
  • For more details on how your data is handled, please review our full Privacy Policy.
